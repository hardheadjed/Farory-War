From the official website: "Story War is a storytelling party game where you fight your friends by referencing pop culture and making stuff up. There are no numbers or rules. The only way to win is to use your card's art and text to convince the judge that your character could defeat your opponent's character in battle." This version of the game is meant to be played over Skype/Google Hangout.
